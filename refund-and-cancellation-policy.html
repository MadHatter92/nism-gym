Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 17 October 2025 (IST)

Owned & operated by Pranshumaan Singh

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to all purchases made on www.nismgym.com (“NISM Gym”). Our offerings are primarily digital courses, live/recorded classes, and educational services.

1) Refunds for Digital Courses & Recordings

  • No Refunds after Access: Once course access is granted (account created, link sent, or content viewed/downloaded), payments are non‑refundable.
  • Duplicate / Failed Payment: If you are charged twice or a failed transaction is settled later, we will issue a full refund for the extra charge after verification.
  • Technical Issues: If you face a platform issue that prevents access and we cannot resolve it within 5 business days, you may request a refund.

2) Live Classes, Webinars & Workshops

  • Rescheduling: If we reschedule, your enrollment will be moved to the next available session or you may request a refund.
  • Cancellation by NISM Gym: If we cancel an event in full, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • No‑Show: Non‑attendance by the learner is not eligible for a refund, but we may provide recordings if available.

3) Order Cancellations

  • Before Access: You may request cancellation before course access is granted; in such cases we will process a full refund.
  • After Access: Cancellations are not possible once access is enabled.

4) Refund Method & Timelines

Approved refunds are initiated to the original payment method within 5–7 business days. Bank or payment gateway processing times may affect when funds appear in your account.

5) How to Request a Refund

Email mail@mayaskara.com with your order ID, registered email/phone, payment screenshot, and a brief description of the issue. Our team will review and respond within 2 business days.

6) Exceptions

  • Promotional or discounted purchases may be non‑refundable unless mandated by law or explicitly stated.
  • Third‑party services or tools bundled with our courses are subject to their respective policies.

7) Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us at mail@mayaskara.com.
